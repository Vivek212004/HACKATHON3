Guidelines

Step 1: Sign Up and Create an Account

  • Visit the Website: Open your web browser and go to Smart Recycle Waste.
  • Sign Up:
    • Click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the homepage.
    • Fill in your details such as name, email address, and create a password.
    • Verify your email address by clicking on the link sent to your inbox.
  • Login: Once verified, log in to your account using your email and password.

Step 2: Complete Your Profile

  • Access Your Profile: Click on your profile icon or name at the top right corner and select "Profile" from the dropdown menu.
  • Fill in Profile Information: Provide additional details such as your address, phone number, and preferred payment method.
  • Set Up Payment Information: Enter your bank account or PayPal details for receiving your earnings.

Step 3: Understand the Recycling Process

  • Learn What Can Be Recycled: Visit the "Recyclable Materials" section to understand which materials can be recycled for money. Common materials include plastics, paper, metals, and electronics.
  • Read Guidelines: Each material has specific guidelines for preparation. Ensure materials are clean and sorted according to the provided instructions.

Step 4: Collect and Sort Your Waste

  • Collect Waste: Start collecting recyclable materials at home, work, or in your community.
  • Sort and Prepare: Sort the materials according to the categories listed on the website. Clean the materials if necessary and pack them appropriately.
Guidelines Continued

Step 5: Schedule a Pickup or Drop-off

  • Request a Pickup: Go to the "Pickup Request" section and schedule a pickup. Enter your address and preferred time slot.
  • Find a Drop-off Location: Alternatively, you can drop off the materials at designated recycling centers. Find the nearest location on the "Drop-off Locations" map.

Step 6: Track Your Earnings

  • Check Your Balance: After your materials are processed, the earnings will be credited to your account. Check your balance in the "Wallet" section.
  • Withdraw Your Earnings: Once you reach the minimum payout threshold, you can withdraw your earnings. Click on the "Withdraw" button and choose your payment method.

Step 7: Maximize Your Earnings

  • Refer Friends: Use your referral code to invite friends and family. Earn a bonus for each successful referral.
  • Participate in Promotions: Keep an eye on the "Promotions" page for special events and bonuses.
  • Stay Informed: Subscribe to the newsletter for updates on new recyclable materials and earning opportunities.
